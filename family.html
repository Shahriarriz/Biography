Family & Friends

My family consists of 4 members. We live as a nuclear family although my relatives live near our area. In the year 2003, my parents shifted to Dhaka from our hometown and started a small business. And that's how I started my life in this city. During the holidays, we go on family trips to our villages and abroad. I haven't got a huge circle of friends but the ones I keep are the best I could get. Though I don't get to spend time with them much, I try to arrange get-togethers once in a while to keep in touch. Their role in my life will always be momentous.
